Media Queries

Dead simple mixins for the masses. Is used extensively in other pojects

Relevant configuation:

Variable Default What it do
$small 30rem When to switch out of small sizing.
$large 60rem When to switch out of medium sizing.
$max-width 60rem The maximum width of the content grid.

Mixins:


@include not-small() {... styles to apply above small width}

@include medium-break() {... styles to apply at between small and large widths}

@include large-break() {... styles to apply above large widths}
